---

## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to IDSMK homepage">
    Visit `https://idsmk.ru` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register an account">
    Click "Sign Up", fill in your email and password, then verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Main of IDSMK">
    Log in and select "Main" from the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
