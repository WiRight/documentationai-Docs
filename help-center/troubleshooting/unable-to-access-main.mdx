---
title: Unable to Access Main of IDSMK
description: Fix common access issues with the Main of IDSMK.
---

## Symptoms

- Cannot load the main dashboard
- Login page keeps refreshing
- "Access Denied" errors

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping idsmk.ru
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and cached files for idsmk.ru.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    Sometimes browser extensions interfere with access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
